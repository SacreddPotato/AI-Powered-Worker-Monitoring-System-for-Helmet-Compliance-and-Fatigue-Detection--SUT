About the Team

Graduation Project Team

A collaboration of 8 students specializing in AI and Computer Vision.

This system was designed and implemented as our second year capstone project. Our goal was to create an automated, non-intrusive safety monitoring solution that could be deployed in industrial environments.

Technical Implementation

Unlike standard off-the-shelf solutions, We are aiming to build our own pipelines to ensure accuracy in challenging conditions.

Our Contributions

  • Architecture: We integrated YOLOv8 for object detection with a Swin Transformer backbone for complex fatigue analysis.
  • Full Stack: The team handled everything from the Python/Flask backend and Model training to this frontend demo dashboard.
